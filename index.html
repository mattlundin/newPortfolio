My name is Matt and I am a front-end developer in Montgomery, Texas and I love bringing websites to life. I may have a slight obsession with animations and transitions. Currently I'm focusing primarily on vanilla JavaScript for functionality while using CSS to make it really pop.

 

I enjoy making websites dance and learning about new interactive ways that I can implement to give visitors an unforgettable experience. From a monitor to a cell phone and everything in between I give websites a seamless transition no matter what screen size it's being viewed on.
